LEVER SIMULATOR

Thats right, your favorite games getting a sequel

Lever Simulator is getting a sequel, we have thought long and hard about what we wanted this sequel to be, You see Lever Simulator by almost all measures is a perfect game, So how, we hear you asking, did we add anything of notable value to the sequel,

NEW FEATURES

  1. firstly we felt Lever Simulator lacked story, we have rectified this and added a whole bible of new story to Lever Simulator 2!
  2. Additionally we have added a new PvE mode, "Buttons strike Back", we haven't figured out all the details yet, but I am fairly certain it will be a political thriller and most likely be an allegory for Battle of Rorke's Drift from the anglo zulu war.
  3. NEW NUMBER: 4
    some may believe it to be against the spirit of the original Lever simulator, but do not fear, we have implemented 4 in a tasteful way that adds to the original rather than ruining it.
  4. BALANCE CHANGES
    2 has been nerfed and 1 has been buffed to encourage a more varied and creative play style
  5. NEW VEHICLE: Hoverbike
    this is an endgame vehicle that is crafted in the Mars forge, requiring a new resource "Zyphirium" abtained in the harsh lava filled wastelands of Zypher; and essence of light, obtained from the light goddess "prisma"
